IV. Chapter Four — On the Method

A stacked ensemble

Three estimators — trained independently on engineered features, then stacked through a meta-learner. Each prediction is the consensus of three separate machines looking at the same patient through different lenses.

Inputs

Engineered features

Eight raw indicators are expanded into forty-three engineered features — metabolic indices, interaction terms, log-transforms.

  • Glucoseraw
  • BMI × Age×
  • Insulin / BMI÷
  • Metabolic Scorez
  • Pedigreeraw
Output

A single risk probability

The meta-learner stacks the three estimator outputs into one calibrated probability, paired with a confidence interval and a cluster assignment.

  • P(diabetes)0–1
  • Confidence%
  • Metabolic zσ
